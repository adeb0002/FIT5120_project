Our Mission

At EcoInsight, we recognise Melbourne's growing environmental awareness and the desire to make a positive impact. Addressing climate change can feel overwhelming and knowing where to start can be difficult. Accurately assessing the environmental impact of your household habits is one of the biggest obstacles, and without clear insights, it’s hard to know if your efforts are making a difference.

EcoInsight bridge's this gap by providing personalised insights into your personal carbon footprint based on local open datasets and statistics, helping you understand the true impact of your daily habits and lifestyle choices.
